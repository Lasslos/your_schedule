<p><i>Untis Connect</i> is an open source tool that shows your Untis timetable.</p>
<p>Why <i>Untis Connect?</i></p>
<ul>
    <li>Set the colors of the subjects yourself</li>
    <li>Hide irrelevant courses</li>
    <li>Beautiful design</li>
    <li>Open source</li>
</ul>
